Home Page

"I rather think that archives exist to keep things safe – but not secret.” -Kevin Young

My philosophy is to work effectively while processing and creating information swiftly. I enjoy the challenge of learning and adapting within a new work environment. Collaboration and teamwork are an important concept of a job because I thrive in a group environment. Additionally, I work well independently and keep myself on task because of self-pacing. Following setbacks, I return to the start of the project, reassess the situation, compartmentalize tasks, and cautiously begin the process again.

Volunteer Projects

During this project club members knit and crocheted hearts for ICU patients and their families during the Covid-19 pandemic.
